~Churn definition

Example:

A customer is churned if they have not made any purchase in the last 90 days after their previous active period.

~CLV target

Example:

Predict customer spend over the next 90 or 180 days.

~Segmentation goal

Example:

Cluster customers using behavioral + value + engagement features.

~Recommendation goal

Example:

Recommend top 5 products a customer is likely to buy next.

~Anomaly goal

Example:

Detect customers with unusual order amount, return rate, or purchase pattern.

~Topic modeling goal

Example:

Identify major review themes and complaint categories.
